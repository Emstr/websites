## Summary

Expanding beyond notaries and financial advisers into high-value professional services sectors. These verticals typically have:
- High client lifetime value
- Often outdated websites
- Strong need for trust/credibility
- Willingness to pay for quality

## Template Designs by Vertical

### Solicitors Template
- **Colors:** Deep navy (#1a365d), Gold accents (#d69e2e), White
- **Style:** Professional, trustworthy, authoritative
- **Icons:** Scales of justice, gavel, document, shield
- **Pages:** Home, Practice Areas, About/Team, Testimonials, Contact, Fee Transparency
- **Features:** Practice area cards, team profiles, testimonial carousel, clear CTAs

### Accountants Template
- **Colors:** Forest green (#276749), Slate (#475569), White
- **Style:** Clean, precise, numbers-focused
- **Icons:** Calculator, chart, briefcase, clock
- **Pages:** Home, Services, About/Team, Resources, Contact
- **Features:** Service grid, partner profiles, industry expertise badges

### Dental Template
- **Colors:** Teal (#0d9488), Soft blue (#3b82f6), White, Light backgrounds
- **Style:** Bright, friendly, clean, trustworthy
- **Icons:** Tooth, smile, checkmark, calendar
- **Pages:** Home, Treatments, About/Team, Pricing, Book Appointment, Contact
- **Features:** Treatment cards, team photos, before/after potential, booking CTA

### Estate Agents Template
- **Colors:** Deep teal (#134e4a), Warm orange (#ea580c), Slate
- **Style:** Property-focused, local expertise, modern
- **Icons:** Home, key, map pin, handshake
- **Pages:** Home, Sales, Lettings, Property Management, Area Guide, Valuation, Contact
- **Features:** Property showcase, area expertise, valuation CTA, local knowledge
