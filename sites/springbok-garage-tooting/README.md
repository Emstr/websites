# Springbok Garage Ltd - Tooting

Award-winning family garage serving Tooting since 1963.

## Business Information

- **Business:** Springbok Garage Ltd
- **Established:** 1963 (60+ years!)
- **Phone:** 020 8672 7788 / 020 8672 6904
- **Address:** 110 Totterdown Street, Tooting, London SW17 8TA
- **Award:** Wandsworth Guardian 'Garage of the Year' 2012

## Services

- MOT Testing (Class 4 & 7)
- Car Servicing (Full & Interim)
- Body Repairs & Accident Damage
- Car Wash & Valeting
- Breakdown & Recovery
- Commercial Vehicles
