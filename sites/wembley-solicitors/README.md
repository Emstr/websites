# Wembley Solicitors

Demo website for Wembley Solicitors - a North West London law firm specializing in Immigration Law, Family Law, and Commercial Leases.

## Business Details

- **Business**: Wembley Solicitors
- **Phone**: 020 3417 3700
- **Email**: info@wembleysolicitors.com
- **Location**: 4 minute walk from Wembley Central Station, North West London

## Services

- **Immigration Law**: Visa applications, appeals, asylum claims, citizenship (Free initial advice online)
- **Family Law**: Divorce, children matters, financial settlements
- **Commercial Leases**: Fixed fees £1,000-£1,500

## Key Features

- **Fixed-Fee Pricing**: £60 consultations, transparent pricing throughout
- **Saturday Hours**: Open Saturdays 10am-2pm (key differentiator!)
- **Convenient Location**: 4 minutes from Wembley Central Station
- **UK-Wide Services**: Remote consultations available
